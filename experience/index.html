Adaptability, Technical Proficiency, and Unwavering Commitment

As a seasoned IT professional with over a decade of experience, I have honed my expertise across a broad spectrum of roles, culminating in my current position as an Enterprise IT Systems Administrator with Cabinetworks Group. My journey began in the bustling environment of Time Warner Cable, where I developed foundational skills and leadership capabilities as a Technical Operation Supervisor. This role laid the groundwork for my subsequent position as Field Engineering Supervisor at Charter Communications, where I further refined my technical acumen and managerial skills. A brief yet impactful stint at Tioga Downs Casino Resort as an IT Specialist provided me with unique insights into IT operations within the hospitality and entertainment industry. Now, working remotely for Cabinetworks Group since May 2021, I leverage my comprehensive background to ensure robust, efficient IT systems administration, underpinning the company's nationwide operations.
